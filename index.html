About Us

Data Science Society at Berkeley (DSSB), supported by the Berkeley Institute of Data Science , aims to create a cross-campus Data Science learning platform for interested students and provide them with the best possible resources for success. This year, besides hosting various projects and research, we are preparing to host an annual Data Science Forum with expected audience size of 800 at UC Berkeley on Saturday April 23rd, 2016. We will be inviting 12 data science leaders from the most innovative companies at Bay Area.
